Mission

Our mission is to optimize the food industry by providing a secure platform that tracks every stage of food production, from farm to fork.

Through blockchain technology, we aim to build trust and confidence in the food supply chain, ensuring consumers have access to reliable information about the origin and handling of their food.

WHY

Food Safety & Quality

Traceability allows the agricultural industry to track food from "farm to fork, ensuring consumers get safe, high-quality products by identifying contamination sources quickly.

Transparency & Trust

By tracking movement through the supply chain, everyone involved gains visibility, building trust with consumers who increasingly demand transparency in their food choices

DePPs empower small farmers

DePPs ensure ALL food, regardless of brand, has a trackable "passport" for origin & and quality

Transparency wins, not labels.

HOW

DePPs gathers data from IoT devices, compliance records, farmers/on-ground teams, and validators, securely storing it on the blockchain for transparent and immutable traceability

JOIN THE MOVEMENT

DePPS representing a paradigm shift for decentralized transparency in the agricultural industry.

WHERE

DePPs is being implemented in countries like Colombia, Indonesia, and China, where the agricultural industry seeks enhanced traceability solutions to meet consumer demand for transparency and safety in food supply chains.

ABOUT FARMSENT

Farmsent is a farmer's blockchain aimed at revolutionizing the way food is traded and ensuring global food security. By leveraging blockchain technology, Farmsent streamlines the food supply chain, reducing the number of intermediaries involved, leading to lower prices, enhanced environmental stewardship, and increased transparency.

With Farmsent, farmers can set their prices and sell directly to consumers, empowering them to earn more and ultimately making farming a more profitable and sustainable profession.
